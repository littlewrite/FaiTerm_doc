
# AI

Click the **▼** menu in the top-right corner to open **AI Chat**.

<img src="/images/screenshots/open_ai_chat.png" width="600" alt="Open AI Chat" />

## Chat mode

Chat mode is the simplest option: you ask questions and the AI answers, without running anything on your machine.

## Auto mode

If you choose **auto** mode, after you send a request the AI analyzes it and may run commands in your environment, for example:
